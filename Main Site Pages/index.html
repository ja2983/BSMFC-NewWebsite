Welcome to Bugbrooke St. Michaels FC

Welcome to the official website of Bugbrooke St. Michaels FC. Established in 1929 and affiliated to the Northamptonshire Football Association, we are an England Accredited Club that has expanded rapidly over the years with ambitions to match. Based in the village of Bugbrooke, we are proud to provide football opportunities for all and now boast seventeen Youth teams ranging from Under 7's to Under 18's along with three Open-Aged teams, which includes a Ladies team. Our ambition is to ensure that player development and enjoyment are abundant in equal measure. All of our teams play and train at The Sett which has a Clubhouse with changing rooms, bar and social area making our facilities amongst the best in the county. We hope that you enjoy browsing our website and please do not hesitate to contact us should you require further information about our club. Thank you for visiting. #COYB
